About Us

Mtokijani Foundation is a non-profit organization based in Tanzania, dedicated to environmental conservation, community empowerment, and sustainable development. We work to protect nature, preserve wildlife, and inspire future generations to care for our planet.

Vision

To create a sustainable and environmentally conscious society that values and protects natural ecosystems for present and future generations.

Mission

To promote environmental conservation, climate change awareness, tree planting, waste management, and sustainable community livelihoods through education, advocacy, and partnerships.

Focus Areas

  • Tree planting and nursery support
  • River and watershed protection
  • Environmental education for schools
  • Waste management and recycling
  • Community livelihood projects
