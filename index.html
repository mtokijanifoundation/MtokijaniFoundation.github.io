Mtokijani Foundation is a Tanzania-based non-profit dedicated to environmental conservation, community empowerment, and sustainable development. Join us as we restore landscapes, protect wildlife, and educate future generations.

Our Causes

Tree Planting Programs

We plant and nurture native trees across Tanzania to restore forests, improve air quality, and create sustainable green spaces for future generations.

Wildlife Protection

Our team works to protect endangered animals and preserve their natural habitats through community involvement and awareness campaigns.

Community Education

We conduct environmental education programs in schools and local communities to inspire sustainable living and conservation habits.

Waste Management

We promote recycling, proper waste disposal, and community clean-up efforts to maintain cleaner, healthier environments.

River Clean-Up Campaigns

We organize regular clean-up drives and awareness initiatives to preserve Tanzania’s rivers and protect aquatic life.

Aquaponic Projects

We introduce aquaponic systems that combine fish farming and vegetable growing, creating efficient, eco-friendly food production methods.

Beekeeping Initiatives

We train local farmers in sustainable beekeeping, supporting pollination, biodiversity, and eco-based livelihoods.

Sustainable Agricultural Practices

We empower farmers with eco-friendly techniques that preserve soil health, reduce chemicals, and increase productivity naturally.

EcoGreen Projects

We develop green technology and renewable energy solutions that drive sustainable community growth and reduce carbon footprints.

Soil Conservation Practices

We promote erosion control and soil restoration to maintain fertile land, secure food supply, and combat desertification.
